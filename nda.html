N.D.A. Summary

The Confidentiality Agreement, effective from November 3rd, 2023, outlines terms between Impact Reality LLC (Impact Lab) and IMPACT LABS PLAY TESTER. It defines "Confidential Information" broadly, including non-public business details like plans, financials, and customer information.

Exclusions from Confidential Information are specified. The Receiving Party's obligations involve protecting and not using disclosed information for purposes other than specified. Compelled Disclosure provisions require the Receiving Party to notify the Disclosing Party if legally compelled to disclose Confidential Information.

The term and termination clause sets the duration of the Agreement, with provisions for returning or destroying Confidential Information upon request. Warranties disclaim any guarantees on Confidential Information. Remedies include specific performance and injunctive relief for breaches.

Dispute resolution mechanisms involve governing laws, jurisdiction, and arbitration. The Agreement covers miscellaneous provisions such as assignment restrictions, notice requirements, the entire agreement clause, modification procedures, severability, and counterparts.
